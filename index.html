About US



Our core objectives expound the all-round development of children so that the values of human dignity and equality are enshrined in the hearts of our children.
Our vision to develop world graduates who are sensitive and resilient, able to navigate life with compassion, objectivity and equanimity; and who will always seek to generate solutions to create a healthy and harmonious society.
Our school will provide for students a dynamic learning environment that is reflective, sensitive, and empowering, and is committed to developing excellence in academics and behaviour
